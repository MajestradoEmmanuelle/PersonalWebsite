about

In life, extraordinary things are bound to happen to us. No matter what, where, when or how they might happen, nobody knows. But they will. Sometimes though, things aren't as extraordinary as they may seem. Sometimes, terrible things happen. But it's okay, that's life. Suck it up. My life has been a roller coaster ride. It still is. It has its ups and its downs, and don't forget the upside downs that somehow I didn't know how I ended up with. Life sucks for everyone. Deal with it. I'm making it sound like it's all sadness and gloom, lol no, it's not. And that's what this blog is for. I'm here to share with you glimpses of my life that I had the privilege to live. I really hope you had as much fun reading about it as I had writing about it. Enjoy! :)
